# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: February 28, 2026**

Quran Player ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the Quran Player app.

## Information We Collect

**We do not collect, store, or share any personal data.**

The app does not require account creation or login. We do not track your location, contacts, or browsing activity.

## Subscription & Payments

All purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not have access to your payment details. Apple's privacy policy governs payment transactions.

## Analytics

We may use third-party analytics tools (such as Adapty) to understand general app usage patterns, such as which features are popular. This data is anonymous and cannot be used to identify you personally.

## Data Storage

Audio files downloaded for offline listening are stored locally on your device. We do not upload or transmit this data.

## Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect any personal information from children. The app is suitable for all ages.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.
